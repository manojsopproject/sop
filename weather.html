Seasonal Farming Advisory
Kharif Season (Jun–Oct) Monsoon onset is crucial. Prepare fields for transplanting rice and sowing maize. Apply pre-emergence herbicides before first rain.
Rabi Season (Oct–Mar) Cool dry winters ideal for wheat, mustard, peas. Irrigate at crown root initiation (CRI) stage for wheat yield boost.
Irrigation Planning Monitor soil moisture weekly. Sandy soils need irrigation every 5–7 days; clay soils every 10–14 days depending on crop stage.
Frost Alert (Dec–Jan) Cover sensitive crops (vegetables, nurseries) with polythene sheets. Avoid light irrigation on frost nights — it worsens damage.
